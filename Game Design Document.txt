Game Design Document

Game concept

Vertical scroller ico hockey platform game. 
Control an ice hocker player and shoot pucks into nets as they come along
Avoid defenders too 

Target Platforms

Cross platform
Browser game

UI/ Player controls

Click and move
Touch and move

Game mechanics

Game constantly moves upwards
Nets will appear from the top of the screen
Defenders will also appear and move towards the player
User clicks and drags the player left and right
To shoot the puck they release the player
If they get hit by a defender they lose

Pixel art style
